Overview

The Seductive Anglers Syndicate (SAS) is less a guild, more a tidal current — pulled by moonlight, laced with secrets, and dressed to shimmer. We don’t fish. We flirt with the unknown. Each cast is a caress. Each ripple? A rumor. Down where the dream-fish drift, we listen... and lure.

Core Features 🧜🏽‍♀️

  • Manual Tease-Casting: Rhythm-based minigame. Weather, moons, and fashion affect finesse.
  • Passive Tidesnaring: Set glimmer traps or hire NPC sirens to vibe while you drift.
  • Siren Hirelings: Give ‘em a rod and they’ll give you a story. Bond grows with each moon-gift.
  • XP & Sway: Unlock deeper waters and rarer catches through charm + curiosity.
  • Legendary Waters: Astral lakes, tide temples, dreamy trenches. Each hums a different tune.

  • Moon Phases: Some fish only flirt back when the moon is watching.

  • Time of Day: Dusk and dawn hold different truths.
  • Flavor-Locked Catches:

    • Phantom Minnow: Glitches out after midnight. Ghost-coded.
