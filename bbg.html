Overview

The Bedrock Baddie Guild (BBG) is the premier mining guild in the Bootyverse. Founded by Ruby and Sapphire after departing the dusty ass ForgeFold guild, BBG is all curve, no compromise. They work with grit, grace, and gorgeous gear—tossing outdated traditions into the dust pile where they belong.

BBG isn’t just about mining. It’s a movement. An aesthetic. A seismic cultural shift.

Vibe & Philosophy

“We don’t dig for treasure. We ARE the treasure.”

  • No hierarchy — respect is earned through contribution.
  • Fashion is encouraged. Cheek confidence required.
  • Combat style: Hands. Just hands.
  • They craft tools; they don’t just wield them.

Founding Context

Born from rebellion, Ruby and Sapphire left ForgeFold after its creativity dulled under outdated hierarchy. The new guild became a radiant counter-movement — a reclamation of mining with heart, intellect, and flair.
